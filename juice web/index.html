Our Story

Born in Inyara,
Built on Freshness

Banena Juice Supply started with a simple belief — that nature's best fruits deserve to be enjoyed in their purest form. Located in the vibrant Inyara neighbourhood of Mbeya, we source ripe mangoes, sweet bananas, and creamy avocados directly from local farmers.

Every glass we serve is squeezed fresh to order. No sugar additives. No artificial flavours. Just honest, wholesome fruit goodness that you can taste and feel.

  • Locally sourced fruits
  • Made fresh on demand
  • Affordable prices
  • Hygienic preparation
